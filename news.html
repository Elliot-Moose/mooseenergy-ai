Moose AI Launches Intelligent Solar Monitoring Platform

We're excited to announce that Moose AI is now accepting early registrations for our solar monitoring platform. Built on over 15 years of hands-on experience in the field, Moose AI brings intelligent, affordable monitoring to solar operators of all sizes — from single rooftop systems to large commercial arrays.

Our platform gives operators clear visibility into their systems around the clock, detecting real issues early and cutting through the noise so teams know exactly what needs attention. No expensive hardware upgrades required — Moose connects to the equipment and data sources you already have.

Solar owners and operators across Ontario can pre-register their systems today at mooseenergy.ai/register. We're onboarding systems on a rolling basis and will be in touch with next steps shortly after registration.

Why We Built Moose AI

After more than 15 years installing and operating solar systems across Ontario, our founding team kept running into the same problem: operators had no reliable way to know when something was actually wrong with their system — until it was too late.

Existing monitoring tools were either too expensive, too noisy, or too generic to be useful in the field. They flagged everything, which meant teams stopped paying attention. Or they missed real issues entirely until a site visit confirmed the damage.

Moose AI was built to fix that. We wanted a monitoring platform that understood what healthy looks like for each individual system — and only spoke up when something genuinely needed attention. Intelligent, accurate, and accessible to anyone running solar, not just enterprise operators with large budgets.

We're proud to be bringing this to market in 2025, and we look forward to helping solar operators get more out of their systems.
