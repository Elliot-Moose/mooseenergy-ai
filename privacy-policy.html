Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2025

Moose AI ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who visit our website at mooseenergy.ai. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide to us:

  • Email address — when you submit your email to join our early access waitlist.
  • Usage data — standard web analytics such as pages visited, browser type, and referring URL. This data is collected in aggregate and is not linked to individuals.

We do not collect names, payment information, or any sensitive personal data through this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • To contact you with updates about the Moose AI platform and early access availability.
  • To understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Waitlist email addresses are stored securely and accessed only by the Moose AI team. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

4. Cookies

This website may use basic session cookies required for normal browser operation. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party cookies at this time.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. By visiting our site, your request is processed by GitHub's servers. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement for details on how they handle visitor data.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent and be removed from our waitlist at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@mooseenergy.ai.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
