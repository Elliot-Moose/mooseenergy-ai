About Us

Built by People Who've Done This Work

Moose AI was founded by the team behind JAZZ Solar Solutions — a renewable energy firm with over 15 years of hands-on experience designing, installing, and operating solar systems across Ontario.

That experience taught us something: the hardest part of solar isn't the hardware — it's knowing when something is wrong, and acting before it gets worse. Moose is our answer to that problem.

We're building intelligent monitoring software that puts the right information in front of the right people at the right time — so solar systems run the way they were designed to.

Founders

Ethan Harrison
